## Introduction

This resource pack is designed for engineering leaders, platform teams, enablement leads, and Copilot champions who want to turn GitHub Copilot from a pilot into a measurable capability. Use it to align leadership on ROI, run a structured rollout, equip developers with best practices, and establish governance that scales.

## How to Use

1. **Start with strategy:** Review the business case and rollout plan with leadership and platform stakeholders.
2. **Run a focused pilot:** Select teams with clear workflows and measurable baselines.
3. **Enable developers quickly:** Use the workshop outline, IDE guides, and exercises for onboarding.
4. **Improve prompt quality:** Share the prompt guide and team-specific examples during office hours.
5. **Measure and report:** Track usage, outcomes, sentiment, and ROI monthly.
6. **Govern responsibly:** Apply admin settings, exclusions, and policy guidance before broad scaling.

## Suggested Adoption Flow

- **Weeks 1-2:** Confirm sponsorship, success metrics, and pilot scope.
- **Weeks 3-4:** Deliver training and launch the pilot.
- **Days 30-60:** Review adoption data, expand to adjacent teams, and refine support.
- **Days 60-90:** Standardize reporting, governance, and champion-led enablement.
